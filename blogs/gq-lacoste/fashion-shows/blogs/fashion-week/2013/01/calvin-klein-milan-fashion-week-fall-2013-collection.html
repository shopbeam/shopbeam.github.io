Calvin Klein Fall 2013: Luxury Gets Athletic

gq.com
January 13, 2013 5:15 am
Facebook
Twitter

Here’s a thought: What if luxury and functionality aren’t mutually exclusive? That’s the question Calvin Klein’s Italo Zucchelli answers with a show that sees utilitarian details merge with rich fabrics and black tie dressing with athletically minded items. Pieces like a gray double-faced cashmere coat came with a four-pocket front while down below, slim, dark, dressy trousers were stocked with clean-laying cargo pockets—all perfect for stowing the modern man’s techie arsenal. It all ads up to a Fall wardrobe with equal footing in the tailored and sportswear arenas.
