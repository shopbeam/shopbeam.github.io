The Summer Shirt Jay Z and Justin Timberlake Made Cool Again

Matt Sebra
August 1, 2013 8:30 am
Facebook
Twitter

The Legends of the Summer are doing more than just entertaining the masses around the globe, they’re also showing guys how to stay cool, while looking damn cool, in the heat. Both Jay Z and JT have been rocking collared short-sleeve shirts on their joint tour, a silhouette most guys avoid thanks to its one-time association with life’s nerdier pursuits. But now, with updated, slimmer fits and modern proportions, the shirts feel fresh on stage in front of thousands or under your unlined blazer at work. Designers are offering them up at all prices, from Timberlake’s Neil Barrett to Jay’s beloved Theory must-have and even easy-on-the-wallet go-to’s like Asos and Topman. So pick one up, button it up, and look sharp no matter what the thermometer says.
