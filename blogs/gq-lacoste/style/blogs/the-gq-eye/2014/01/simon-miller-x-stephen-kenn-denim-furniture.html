Simon Miller and Stephen Kenn Collaborate on Furniture That'll Make Your Jeans Jealous

Jian DeLeon
January 29, 2014 7:56 am
Facebook
Twitter

Furniture designer Stephen Kenn has fast become a favorite of menswear industry insiders like Todd Snyder, Nick Wooster, and J. Crew’s Frank Muytjens. Kenn’s furniture combines a penchant for rugged fabrics like vintage tents and rugged steel frames with an air of modern minimalism. Now, he’s teaming up with Simon Miller for a capsule collection inspired by denim.

It used to be that your jeans ruined your furniture, but this collaboration between Stephen Kenn and Simon Miller runs the risk of messing up your jeans. Mixing oxidized copper frames with indigo cotton canvas and hand-dyed belts, the deep blue seats recall your favorite pair of denim. It isn’t just the armchair and sofa that are available, but all of Stephen Kenn’s Inheritance collection, which is available in the Simon Miller x Stephen Kenn indigo finish by special order. The armchair will set you back $2,800, and while the sofa costs $6,000. Pre-orders are currently being taken at shop.stephenkenn.com.
