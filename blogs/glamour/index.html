How to Wear a Classic Camel Coat in a New, Cool, and Edgy Way

by Fawnia Soo Hoo
Fashion writer. Sample sale habitué, television addict and self-admitted Anglophile.
Share
3
Comments
3

We've had fall coats on our minds ever since Danica showed us five amazing (and affordable) oversize styles that we need to put on our list. And then newly minted style star Lily Collins wore a camel coat out over the weekend, teaching us yet another lesson on fall coats—a classic cut can totally be updated for the new season.

Now, every girl needs a good toggle coat, which is perfect to wear over a suit for work and a dress for formal occasions, but Lily is showing us how versatile the outerwear classic can be. She wore her menswear-inspired peak-lapel overcoat in a breezy, edgy way with a few easy style tricks. First, she rolled the sleeves up to create an impromptu contrast cuff. Then layered it over a cropped graphic tee (very Cara Delevingne), skinny black jeans, and killer heels. Mini Passage Calf Hair Crossbody Bag (and Dior Beauty Rouge Dior Lipstick) bring the whole outfit together by bridging the gap between designer chic and rock 'n' roll edge.
